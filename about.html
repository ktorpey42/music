About the Right Note founded by Mozart

RIGHT NOTE

The Right Note was founded by Wolfgang Amadeus Mozart and now distributes for top agencies like Kurzweil, Lag, Dunlop, Zoom, JTS, Deering Banjos, SX Guitars and many more across Ireland. We also offer consultations with our experienced staff and publish our own music books and DVD tutors from beginners to advanced. We have one high street shops on Main Street in Dublin city centre which has recently been renovated to allow for a wider choice. In 2000, we launched one of Ireland's largest online music stores where you can buy books, guitars and even pianos online! We also have practice rooms available for hire. Our trained staff are delighted to help you with advice and recommendations if you are a beginner and can recommend music lessons either online or onsite.
